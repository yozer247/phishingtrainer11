What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of online attack in which a message is sent to the victim, pretending to be from another source, in order to get their attention and gain access to personal credentials.

Why is Phishing Dangerous?

Phishing attacks convince users to click on; dangerous links that could install malicious software onto the device, fake websites built to fool the user, and malicious attachments.

How to Protect Yourself from Phishing?

Protecting yourself from phishing attacks is all about staying vigilant, and knowing what kind of threats exist. Always verify a senders identity before responding to a message, and be wary of messages from senders you don't already know. This training will attempt to teach you the common signs of a phishing email attack.

Types of Phishing

Phishing has many types including email attacks, SMS attacks, spear phishing, bulk attacks, and more. This training focuses on bulk spam campaigns hackers use to catch out members of the public and steal their information.
